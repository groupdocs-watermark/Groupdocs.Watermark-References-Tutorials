## How to create tamper‑resistant watermark in PowerPoint presentations?

Tamper‑resistant watermarks are designed to stay visible even when a user tries to edit, copy, or export a slide. By leveraging GroupDocs.Watermark’s built‑in security features—such as watermark anchoring, opacity control, and protection flags—you can ensure that your branding or confidentiality notice cannot be removed without breaking the presentation.
